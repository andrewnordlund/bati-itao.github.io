Training and Coaching 2023

The purpose of the training is to develop and create accessible documents and applications as well as deepen our knowledge of adaptive technological methods used by people with functional limitations.

Did you know that many other learning opportunities are offered by the College@ESDC? Use the Course Finder tool Internal on iService to find those best suited for you! In the Course Finder subject field, select ‘Diversity, Accessibility and inclusion’. For more information or for accessible methods of registration please contact the College@ESDC: nc-college-gd@servicecanada.gc.ca.

Apriltraining and coaching sessions

The IT Accessibility Office (ITAO) is pleased to offer Courses on ‘Introduction to Digital Accessibility’, ‘Creating accessible Excel documents using Microsoft 365’, ‘Creating accessible content using Microsoft 365’, and ‘creating accessible PDF documents.

These courses are offered in both French & English. Please refer to the details below to register:

Introduction to Digital Accessibility

Learn about different types of barriers that the digital environment may create for people with different abilities and why inclusive design eliminates them. This training contains concepts and not technical subjects.

  • French session
    April 4, 2023 | 10:30 AM to 11:30 AM (Eastern Daylight Time)
    Introduction à l'Accessibilité Numérique Interne
  • English session
    April 4, 2023 | 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM (Eastern Daylight Time)
    Introduction to Digital Accessibility Internal

Creating accessible Excel documents using Microsoft 365

Learn good practices for creating accessible content with Excel using Microsoft 365.

Note: Introduction to Digital Accessibility is strongly recommended as a prerequisite.

  • English session
    April 18, 2023 | 1:30 PM to 3:00 PM (Eastern Daylight Time)
    Creating accessible Excel documents using Microsoft 365 Internal
  • French session
    April 19, 2023 | 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM (Eastern Daylight Time)
    Création de documents Excel accessibles à l'aide de Microsoft 365 Interne

Creating accessible content using Microsoft 365

Learn good practices for creating accessible content with Outlook, Word, and PowerPoint using Microsoft 365.

Note: Introduction to Digital Accessibility is strongly recommended as a prerequisite.

  • English session
    April 20, 2023 | 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM (Eastern Daylight Time)
    Creating accessible content using Microsoft 365 Internal

Create accessible PDF documents

Learn how to remediate a PDF document into an accessible format using Foxit PDF Editor. This course is for participants with knowledge about how to create PDF files and know how to use Foxit PDF Editor.

Note: Foxit is required to follow this course. Participants can obtain it through a request to the National Service Desk Internal prior to taking the course.

  • English session
    April 25, 2023 | 1:30 PM to 3:00 PM (Eastern Daylight Time)
    Create accessible PDF documents Internal
