Accessibility Awareness

The IT Accessibility Office provides accessibility awareness and information services. On this page, you will find a list of awareness days, trainings, events and articles related to accessibility.

Awareness Days

Across the country, several awareness days are celebrated throughout the year. The purpose of these days is to inform and raise employee’s awareness of important humanitarian, cultural and social issues around the world.
